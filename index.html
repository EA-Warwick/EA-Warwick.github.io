Who are we?

EA Warwick is all about building a community of students passionate about utilising evidence and reason to tackle the world’s most pressing problems. We are open to all from those who have only just discovered their interest in spending their time, money and worklife having the biggest impact on the world around us, to those who are veteran effective altruists and are looking for a local EA group at Warwick university.
You will be provided the opportunity to engage with a diverse range of problems such as; global poverty, climate change, AI safety, biosecurity, animal welfare, and any other global issue you can think of! These opportunities will be presented in the way of talks by guest speakers who are experts in their field, hackathons, career workshops, weekly reading groups, social events, and networking events. We strive to provide a space in which all are encouraged to learn about effective giving, high-impact career paths, and the latest research on global priorities and take first steps towards having an impact.
If any of this sounds like it could be for you, feel free to get in contact or join us to contribute to solving humanity’s greatest challenges during your time at Warwick!

Specific Areas of Interest

In addition to more general EA topics of discussion we have dedicated officers for each of the following.

  • Biosecurity
  • AI Safety

For each of these topics we aim to run at least 2 talks/events in each of term 1 and 2, usually in collaboration with other societies and almost always with some free food. If any of these topics pique your interest, or you just want to learn more about hot topics in the EA community, we'd love to see you our events in these areas!
