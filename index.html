Who are we?

EA Warwick is all about building a community of students passionate about utilising evidence and reason to tackle the world’s most pressing problems. We are open to all, whether you are new to effective altruism or are a veteran looking for a local group!
You will be provided the opportunity to engage with a diverse range of problems such as; global poverty, climate change, AI safety, biosecurity, animal welfare, and any other global issue you can think of!
We strive to provide a space that encourages all to learn about effective giving, high-impact career paths, and the latest research on global priorities, taking the first steps towards having an impact.
Feel free to get in contact or join us to contribute to solving humanity’s greatest challenges during your time at Warwick!

Specific Areas of Interest

In addition to more general EA topics of discussion we have dedicated officers for each of the following.

  • Biosecurity
  • AI Safety

For each of these topics we aim to run at least 2 talks/events in each of term 1 and 2, usually in collaboration with other societies and almost always with some free food. If any of these topics pique your interest, or you just want to learn more about hot topics in the EA community, we'd love to see you our events in these areas!
