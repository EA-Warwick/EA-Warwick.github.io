What do we do?

Fellowships & Reading Groups

We host a variety of reading groups, from the Introductory Fellowship, to deeper dives into specific topics, such as AI Safety and Rationality.

Co-working & Socials

Coworking is a place to connect with other EAs, collaborate on projects, or just a quiet space to work independently. Held every Weds from 4-6pm in FAB3.28.
We also hold some laid back socials such as coffee mornings, picnics and groups runs!

Talks & Workshops

Learn from experts in their field, covering a variety of topics from AI to biosecurity to global development.
We also run occaisional hackathons and career workshops.
